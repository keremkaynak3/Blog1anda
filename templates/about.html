Academic

I started coding when I was in high school. Over the years I would be an expert if I could cover my interest and focus but I am not. That is why I want to tell my story to inspire and motivate you to keep coding. In the very beginning I was learning Python. I watched youTube videos and practiced them until my motivation ended. In 2020, before I started Computer Engineering, I wanted to make my own GAP Year because of pandemic. I was improving myself both English and Coding but with 1 difference. I have started to using C with recommendation one of my lovely friend of my brother. As I heard and experienced, C based languages preferred in Universities. Thus, I started to develop my simple applications on C. But I had a big mistake, I wasted much time trying to learn syntax. I would never...anyway And then... I started first grade and suddenly started to developing at Java. I did not have difficulty but I must admit that I did not improve either.
